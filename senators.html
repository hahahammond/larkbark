Senator Tweet Tracking

This summer I completed a course at Simmons on social network analysis (LIS-505L-BL01). The class was taught by a visiting faculty member from Yonsei University in Seoul, and several of my classmates were Yonsei students who had traveled to Boston for the summer. The course covered theory and methodology… but, more importantly, I learned what bracken is and how to survive the Seoul Metropolitan Subway at rush hour.


For my final project, I analyzed and visualized tweets sent between U.S. senators during Summer 2017 using Python and UCINET – slides below!
